Welcome to Indus Tubes Limited

Indus Tubes Limited, established in 1987, is a reputable manufacturer of high-quality pipes and tubes. Founded by visionary leaders Sh. G. K. Jaju, Sh. I. P. Jain, Sh. B. L. Gupta, and Sh. Kailash Jajodia, the company began with a single mill and has since grown into a major player in the industry with four fully equipped manufacturing units. The company has earned recognition for its state-of-the-art facilities and unwavering commitment to quality, making it a trusted name among government, semi-government, and private corporations.

Infrastructure and Facilities

Indus Tubes Limited has expanded its infrastructure significantly since its inception. The four mills are equipped with modern facilities, ensuring high production efficiency and superior quality.
